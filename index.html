About me

Hello! My name is Christabell. As owner and creative director of my company, I carefully hand-craft most of the Cristalicious designs I create for your event. This allows me to fully customize your event décor, transforming your space from a bare, unadorned room to the vision of beauty, drama, or businesslike practicality your event demands. My goal is to create the precise atmosphere you envision, in any color or style that strikes your fancy. As I collaborate with my trusted creative team – a talented team with whom I’ve built a close working relationship over the past four years – an artistic synergy develops that allows us to assemble the most up-to-the-minute designs to keep your event on the cutting edge of modern décor.
